Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our tree services in Pocatello.

On average, tree removal and other related services could cost around $500. Factors that influence pricing include the tree's height, proximity to power lines or properties, and ease of access. We provide one of the most reasonably priced tree services in Pocatello, so call us now for your free estimate!

Generally, tree trimming is cheaper than full removal because it takes less time, requires less rigging, and does not require stump grinding. For very large or hazard-adjacent trees, complete removal requires meticulous protection of surrounding properties, which increases safety rigging costs.

A tree should be removed if it shows signs of decay (hollow trunk), has infectious fungal growths, exhibits severe root damage, or leans dangerously toward structures. Our arborist can perform a hazard assessment and help you determine the safest course of action.
