# AI SEO Skill

This skill outlines how to leverage Google Search Console (GSC) API performance data and local citations to identify rank-climbing opportunities and execute optimizations.

## Strategic Workflow

1. **Find Striking-Distance Keywords:**
   - Look for queries with high impressions but average position between 11 and 30 (Page 2 / Page 3).
   - These represent low-hanging fruit where minor optimizations can push the page to Page 1.

2. **Optimize CTR (Click-Through Rate):**
   - Target queries with low CTR despite decent average position.
   - Refine the page title to lead with the exact query.
   - Add numbers (e.g. `25+ Years`), action verbs, or trust indicators (`Fully Insured`, `Emergency Response`) to the title and meta description.

3. **GSC Query Filtering:**
   - Always filter performance queries by country (`usa` ISO code) to remove international noise and get precise local rankings.

4. **Off-Page Local Signals:**
   - Build NAP (Name, Address, Phone) citation authority on local directories like Yelp, Loc8NearMe, and yellowpages to match your Google Business Profile (GBP).
